Overnight Oats

The perfect breakfast meal for loafers

It’s one of the easiest, no-cook breakfast options that will leave you with healthy grab-and-go breakfasts to enjoy throughout the week.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Whey protein
  • Raspberry
  • Blueberry
  • Blackberry

Directions

  1. Stir it all together. In a small jar, stir together the oats, milk, whey protein, and chia seeds
  2. It’s soaking time. Place the jar in the fridge to let the oats soak for at least 2 hours, though overnight is best.
  3. Add toppings (berries) and serve. The next morning, add your favorite toppings and enjoy! Alternatively, you can scoop the oats out into a bowl and add toppings from there.
