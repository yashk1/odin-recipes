Breakfast - Overnight oats

Description

Overnight oats are a healthy and easy breakfast option that can be made in advance and eaten cold or at room temperature. They are made by mixing rolled oats with water or milk and letting it sit overnight in the refrigerator. The oats soak up the liquid and become soft and slightly gelatinous, developing a unique texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 120g cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 10g brazilian nuts
  • 30g whey protein
  • 60g raspberry
  • 60g blueberry
  • 60g blackberry

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the rolled oats, chia seeds, brazilian nuts, and whey protein
  2. Add the unsweetened almond milk to the bowl and mix well.
  3. Add whey protein and berries to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Cover the bowl and let it sit overnight in the refrigerator.
  5. In the morning, remove the lid and enjoy your overnight oats cold or at room temperature.
