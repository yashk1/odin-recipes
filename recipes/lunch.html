Chicken Curry

Satisfy your Indian tastebuds cuz Indian food is top tier

It’s one of the easiest, no-cook breakfast options that will leave you with healthy grab-and-go breakfasts to enjoy throughout the week.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup water
  • 2½ cup white Rice
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp ginger root
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp whole cumin seed
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garam Masala
  • 3 lb chicken breast, boneless and skinless
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3½ tbsp ghee clarified butter
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1 medium (2-1/2" dia) onion
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 15 oz tomatoes, crushed, canned
  • 1 cup yogurt, plain whole milk

Directions

  1. Start by chopping onion, garlic and ginger finely.
  2. Heat oil in a pot over medium-high heat, and add diced chicken breasts - cooking until golden brown on both sides, flipping once. Season with salt, and set aside. The chicken does not need to be fully cooked here, just browned all over.
  3. After removing the chicken, add onions, garlic, and ginger - cooking for 3 minutes until tender and translucent. Stir in spices, and 1 tbsp of water. Stir together, then add tomatoes, yogurt, 1/2 cup of water, and chopped cilantro. Bring to a boil, and add chicken back in.
  4. Cover and simmer chicken until cooked through, about 15 minutes.
  5. To serve, squeeze some fresh lemon juice on top, and serve with rice.
